Wedding Photography

Capturing love stories with timeless elegance and cultural beauty

Romantic Couple Poses

Tender, intimate moments that capture the deep connection between couples. These poses emphasize emotion, love, and the special bond you share.

Forehead Kiss

A tender forehead kiss creates an intimate, peaceful moment that shows protection and deep affection. Perfect for sunset or golden hour shots.

Pro Tip:

Close your eyes for natural emotion. Works beautifully with backlighting to create a romantic glow.

Walking Together

Natural walking shots capture authentic joy and connection. These candid moments show your relationship in motion.

Pro Tip:

Look at each other while walking, not the camera. Laugh naturally for genuine expressions.

Gazing Into Eyes

Deep eye contact creates powerful, emotional portraits that showcase the love and connection between partners.

Pro Tip:

Stand close, faces angled slightly toward each other. Focus on the emotion in your eyes.

Silhouette Embrace

Dramatic silhouettes against sunset or bright backgrounds create artistic, timeless images that focus on your connection.

Pro Tip:

Position yourselves between the camera and a bright light source. Hold the pose steady for sharp silhouettes.

Traditional South Indian

Cultural Wedding Poses

Honor your heritage with traditional South Indian wedding poses that celebrate cultural beauty, family traditions, and sacred moments of your ceremony.

Temple Blessings

Sacred moments at the temple with traditional poses showing reverence and cultural beauty. Perfect for pre-wedding or ceremony shots.

Cultural Note:

Respect temple customs. Coordinate with priests for appropriate timing and positioning during ceremonies.

Mangalsutra Ceremony

The sacred moment of tying the mangalsutra, capturing the groom's hands as he places this symbol of marriage around the bride's neck.

Cultural Note:

Focus on the hands and expressions. This is one of the most important moments in South Indian weddings.

Agni Pradakshina

The seven sacred rounds around the holy fire, each step representing vows and promises for your life together.

Cultural Note:

Capture each round from different angles. The fire creates beautiful lighting for dramatic shots.

Traditional Jewelry Focus

Close-up shots highlighting intricate South Indian bridal jewelry, mehndi designs, and traditional attire details.

Pro Tip:

Use macro lens for jewelry details. Natural lighting brings out the gold's warm tones beautifully.

Creative Modern Poses

Contemporary and artistic poses that blend traditional elements with modern photography techniques for unique, Instagram-worthy shots.

Mirror Reflections

Creative use of mirrors, water reflections, or glass surfaces to create unique perspectives and artistic compositions.

Pro Tip:

Clean mirrors before shooting. Experiment with angles to avoid photographer's reflection while capturing the couple.

Fabric Motion

Dynamic shots with flowing dupatta, sari, or veil creating movement and drama in the frame.

Pro Tip:

Use a fast shutter speed to freeze motion, or slower speeds for artistic blur. Have an assistant help with fabric.

Elevated Perspectives

Drone shots or high-angle perspectives that show the couple in beautiful locations with geometric patterns.

Pro Tip:

Use leading lines in architecture or nature. Plan for golden hour light for warm, romantic tones.

Night Photography

Romantic evening shots with city lights, fairy lights, or natural lighting creating magical atmospheres.

Pro Tip:

Use tripod for stability. Combine ambient light with gentle flash fill for perfectly exposed subjects.
